About

Synathra Lab is a public method and product laboratory.

It develops computational event-chain models for systems where events, decisions, obligations and pressures form explainable risk paths.

Positioning

The first public proof case is Synathra.Sport because football is an observable event system with fast risk accumulation. The strategic direction is broader: finance, contract risk, insurance, banking, crypto/stablecoin risk and operational risk.
