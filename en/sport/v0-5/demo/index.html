Demo logic

What the demo shows.

The demo does not forecast final match scores. It shows whether the same frozen event-chain risk score ranks short-horizon danger across related football domains.

event stream chain context frozen score future_goal_h10 risk explanation

Primary metric

Future-goal H10 ranking evidence.

The primary public demo metric is future_goal_h10. A top10_lift near 5 means that the highest-scored 10 percent of events contains roughly five times the future-goal rate of the average event population.
